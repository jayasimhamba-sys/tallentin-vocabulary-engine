## ESCO status
ESCO parser support is included, but ESCO data is not bundled here. ESCO download requires selecting a package, accepting the privacy statement, entering an email address, and receiving a download link.

## Market sources
LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, ATS pages, and company career pages are market-signal references only unless licensed or permissioned. Do not scrape or copy proprietary databases.
